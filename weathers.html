Weathers

In our planet, there's different types of weather which sometimes affect our mood, the place, or even our activities according to what's happening around us. Temperature is also affected by weather.
Here's some examples, which I'll discuss afterwards.

Weather Feeling Opinion
Rainy Nostalgic Makes me lazy
Sunny Optimistic It's nice until the sun becomes unbearable
Cloudy Calm My favorite type of weather
Snowy Awe I saw snow once, and it was beautiful

Rainy

When the weather is rainy , water falls down from the clouds as a rain, like the one that we use to take showers.
It can be light, moderate or heavy, and it can cause floods if the place where rain falls isn't prepared for it.

    Fun facts about rain

  • It has a smell.
  • Rain can worsen Asthma
  • Its formed by rain droplets from clouds, that condense into each other and grow until they get heavy enough to fall down.

Rain-related terms

Rain
The water droplets that fall from high places, like the sky.
Condensation
It's the water collected as droplets on a cold surface when humid air is in contact with it.
Flood
What happens when too much water is concentrated in one place, like a city. Floods can bring sicknesses and pests with them.

Sunny

When the weather is sunny , you can see the sun in the sky, and there's few or no clouds covering it.
Sometimes, temperature is affected by the sun itself, so a Winter day can be warm if the sun shines brightly enough.

    Fun facts about sunny days

  • The sunniest place of Earth is Yuma, Arizona, while the least sunny place of Earth is the South Pole.
  • People prefer sunny days to do outdoor activities, like having a picnic.

Sun-related terms

Sun
The round star that's close to our planet. It's what gives us warmth.
Warmth
The feeling we get when there's high temperatures around us.

Cloudy

When the weather is cloudy , clouds cover up the sun and we can barely see it.

    Fun facts about cloudy days

  • Sometimes, a cloudy day preceedes rain.
  • Cloudy days can be windier than sunny days.

Cloud-related terms

Cloud
The fluffy things that decorate the sky sometimes. They have many shapes.
Storm cloud
The dark clouds that come before rain arrives. They can appear in cloudy days.

Snowy

When the weather is snowy , snow falls down from the clouds just like rain does.
It can be light, moderate or heavy, and it can make entire cities go on hold if too much snow falls down.

    Fun facts about snowy weather

  • Due to low temperatures, you can get sick more easily in snowy days than days with other weathers.

Snow-related terms

Snow
The solid form of water, it falls down from the clouds. Technically, it's just small pieces of ice.
